Support-Us

DONOR

The donors are the users who donates the unutilised medicines stored in their homes through the web-application. They donate medicines for the people who can't afford these expensive medicines. For donating medicines they first need to register themselves on the app.Then they provide the details of the medicines they want to donate. Donating of illegal drugs is not entertained and if found,severe actions will be taken.

TRANSPORTER

Transporter are those people who collect medicines from those donors who want to donate medicines but unable to come to our centres for submitting them.People who are interested in doing social service can work as an agent. They can collect medicines from those people who lies in between their daily route and then submit them at the centres.

MEDICINE STOCK CENTRES

Medicine Stock Centres are the hospitals and pharmaceutical shops who keeps the stock of medicines donated by the people. The donators have to submit the medicines at these centres directly or through the agents. The people who want medicines have to come to these centres along with their BPL card for taking medicines.
