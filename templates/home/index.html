WHAT WE DO


Our platform connects patients to the surplus medicines at homes in a convenient way.
We collect the unused medicines from individuals. Unexpired medicines are then delivered to the needed ones and the expired medicines are then disposed in a correct way to avoid any kind of environmental pollution. The medicines without name or expiry date or other credentials must also be disposed so that it doesn't harm anyone.
Life of a human being is very precious which must be saved at any cost. Here at Patient Sakhi we pride ourselves in delivering unexpired surplus medicines to low-income patients in an efficient way and in minimum possible time so that their valuable lives can be saved.

WHAT TO DO WITH MEDICINES ???

  • Medicines :

    1. One should not keep unused medicines at homes.
    2. Donate the surplus medicines lying in the drawers of your home and awoid their wastage.
    3. Anyone can receive these donated medicines free of cost
    4. Expired medicines are of no use so they should be disposed off to avoid environmental pollution.

  • Donate Medicines :

    1. Do not keep unused medicines at homes.
    2. Donate the unused medicines lying at your homes.
    3. Dispose the medicines in a correct way.
    4. Donating unused mdicines to low-income patients can save their valuable life.

  • Expired Medicines :

    1. Proper drug disposal should be done correctly to avoid contamination of the water or create a hazard for children or pets.
    2. Return the expired medicines to the nearby pharmacies if possible.
    3. Add sawdust,kitty litter or coffee grounds with medication to the plastic bag.Seal the plastic bag and put it in the trash
    4. Remove and destroy all identifying personal information on the prescription label.

  • Medicines without label :

    1. Medicines without the name or expiry date on it are of no use.So dispose them off safely.
    2. Never eat these medicines as it may be possible that u eat them instead of other medicine which looks similar.
    3. Keep these medicines out of the reach of children.
    4. Return these expired medicines to nearby pharmacies.

Keep Medicines Safe at home

  • Always keep the medicines inside a closed cabinet.
  • Try to store medications and vitamins in a cool, dry place away from bright windows or storage
    rooms affected by outdoor weather.
  • For your own safety, store medications only in their original containers.
  • Keep medicines out of the reach of small children to avoid any accident.
  • Large quantities of unused medicines in the house can invite illness. It is advised to keep smaller packs.
